Fashion Illustration

This week’s Featured Book

Fashion Illustration & Design Methods and Techniques for Achieving Professional Results

Versace's chief illustrator teaches us how to draw, halfway between fashion sketch and illustration, in a non-classical and non-stereotypical way. A design tutorial by Daniela Bambatti, Versace's chief (design) illustrator, the book is an idea of couturier Favio Marconi who, impressed by her drawing style and the precision with which she captures the flow of fabric over the body, convinced her to put it into print. Here Bambrilla reveals her fresh and innovative style, halfway between fashion sketching and illustration.

Children's Fashion Illustration

Children's Wear Fashion Illustration Resource Book is a practical aid for fashion drawing. There are 40 figure drawing templates for four different age groups, complemented with multiple sketches and illustrations.

Find out more

Colour in Fashion Illustration

This book is a practical manual intended specifically for anyone interested in delving into the technique of granting colour to fashion figurine illustrations to give them more life and expressiveness.

Find out more

Fashion Drawing

Fashion Drawing is a comprehensive, practical guide to fashion drawing for students. The author covers men, women and children, starting with how to draw the fashion figure and continuing with garments and garment details, before teaching the reader how to render various types of fabric.

Find out more

Modern Fashion Illustration

Modern Fashion Illustration is a how-to book that offers step-by-step the art of fashion illustration, and how to promote it in today's world of social media. It also includes a collection of whimsical fashion illustrations by the author, featuring illustrations waiting to be colored in by novices and practiced illustrators alike.
